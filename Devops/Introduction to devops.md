
### **Interview Q&A (Very Short)**

**1. What is SDLC?**

* Software development life cycle from idea to release.

**2. SDLC phases?**

* Requirement, Design, Development, Testing, Deployment, Maintenance.

**3. Why DevOps?**

* Faster delivery and better collaboration.

**4. DevOps key goal?**

* Automate build, test, deploy.

**5. What is Waterfall?**

* Linear, one-phase-after-another model.

**6. Drawback of Waterfall?**

* Changes are hard and costly.

**7. What is Agile?**

* Iterative development in small cycles.

**8. Agile key concepts?**

* Sprints, feedback, continuous improvement.

**9. Agile vs Waterfall (main difference)?**

* Agile is flexible, Waterfall is rigid.

**10. Why Agile is mostly used?**

* Faster feedback and easy change handling.

---

### **Short Summary (To Remember)**

* **SDLC** → Process to build software
* **DevOps** → Speed + automation + collaboration
* **Waterfall** → Fixed steps, no flexibility
* **Agile** → Small releases, fast feedback, change-friendly

### **Interview Questions & Answers**

* **What is DevOps?**
  Collaboration of Dev + Ops to deliver software faster.

* **Why DevOps is needed?**
  To reduce deployment time and failures.

* **What problem does DevOps solve?**
  Gap between development and operations teams.

* **DevOps in SDLC?**
  Present in build, test, deploy, monitor stages.

* **Is DevOps a tool?**
  No, it’s a culture and practice.

* **Agile vs DevOps?**
  Agile focuses on development, DevOps on delivery.

---

### **Ultra-Short Summary**

* **DevOps** → Faster, automated, reliable releases
* **Goal** → CI/CD + teamwork
* **Used because** → Speed, quality, scalability
